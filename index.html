Features

Everything you need to turn chats into bookings

AI Chat & FAQ
Our assistant answers common questions (pricing, services, policies) using your guidelines. It recognizes intent, gathers details, and moves the conversation toward a confirmed booking — without human hand-offs.
Real-time Availability & Booking
Show actual time slots, collect name/email/phone, and confirm instantly. Bookings sync to your calendar and the customer’s, with automatic reminders and rescheduling links.
Payments & No-Show Protection
Collect deposits or full payments via Stripe to reduce no-shows. PCI compliant by design; you never touch card data.
Omnichannel Reminders
Email and SMS confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups increase attendance and repeat bookings — fully automated.
Security & Privacy
Role-based access, audit logs, data retention controls, and encryption in transit and at rest. Your data, your rules.
